    About PetPet

    Our project main idea is to create a website for rescuing animals from cruelty and neglect (specially dogs & cats). This means shelters and rescues are full of loving, spayed or neutered, vaccinated, and often trained pets who are just waiting to meet you! By adopting a cat or dog from a shelter or rescue, you can rest assured that you have not supported the puppy mill industry. Adopting a pet from a shelter or rescue is a win-win, for you and the pet. So, what are you waiting for? Begin your search for cats and dogs available for adoption near you today.
